Level 1

It is an ideal place to start for families. It's the novice level with small waves, few obstructions, and calmer waters.

Level 2

Intermedium Water Rafting have bigger waves and bigger features than level 1 rapids. There are usually fewer paths a guide can take through a level 2 rapid because of obstructions in the river.

Level 3
